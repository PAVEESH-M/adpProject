Happy Teachers Day !

Teachers' Day is a unique day observed globally to recognise and respect the hard work of educators who shape the minds and futures of students. Teachers' Day is known as a significant event in India ,since it honours the birthday of Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, who was the second President of India and was a highly regarded scholar and philosopher whose contributions to education have inspired generations of students. As we celebrate Teachers' Day in 2024, it's a good time to consider the significant influence teachers have on our lives and to give thanks for their steadfast commitment.
