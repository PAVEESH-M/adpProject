OUTPUT

  • The output unit sends the processed results to the user using output devices such as video monitor, printer, plotter, etc. In computer architecture, output is the information that a computer or device displays, prints, or transmits to another device. Output can be in the form of text, graphics, images, sound, or video.

  • Output devices include: monitors, printers, speakers, projectors, and headphones.

  • The process of communication between input devices and output devices is called input/output (I/O). The central processing unit (CPU) executes the signal instructions from input and output devices, allowing machines to share data.
